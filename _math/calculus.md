# Gradient

\\[ \nabla f = \begin{pmatrix} \frac {\partial f}{\partial x_1} \\ \vdots \\ \frac {\partial f}{\partial x_n} \end{pmatrix} \\]

Gradient vector is perpendicular to countour lines.
